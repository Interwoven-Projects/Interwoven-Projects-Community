## Welcome to Interwoven Projects

We are a growing community of **professionals and hobbyists** across coding, physics, history, AI, philosophy, biology, design, writing, and more.

Our goal is simple:
- Anyone can propose a project.
- Others can join, contribute in their area of strength.
- Everyone who meaningfully participates gets **clear, public credit** for their role in bringing the idea to life.

This repository is the central hub for discovering, proposing, and tracking collaborative projects that span multiple fields.

---

## How It Works

1. **Propose** — Open a structured Project Proposal (see below). Describe the idea, which fields it touches, and what success looks like.
2. **Discuss & Form a Team** — Use GitHub Discussions and Issues. People express interest, clarify scope, and self-organize.
3. **Execute** — Break work into contributions. Use issues, pull requests (when code/docs), or simple updates in the project thread.
4. **Credit & Share** — Every significant contribution is recorded. Finished work is celebrated with clear attribution.
5. **Iterate or Archive** — Projects can evolve, pause, or complete. Everything stays discoverable.

The whole process is designed to be **low-friction** while protecting trust through transparent credit.

## Propose a New Project

The best way to start is with a **structured proposal**.

**→ [Open a Project Proposal](https://github.com/Interwoven-Projects/Interwoven-Projects-Community/issues/new?template=project-proposal.yml)**

Our proposal form asks for:
- Core disciplines involved
- Clear goals and definition of success
- Roles/skills you're looking for
- Rough timeline
- Your thoughts on how contributors should be credited

Proposals are public and become the seed for community discussion.

## Attribution & Credit

Fair credit is core to this community.

Every active project maintains a clear record of who contributed what. We use standardized roles that work across disciplines (Originators, Domain Experts, Researchers, Writers, Developers, Reviewers, Facilitators, etc.).

**Read the full system:** [ATTRIBUTION.md](./ATTRIBUTION.md)

We believe good attribution:
- Builds trust
- Encourages people to contribute their best work
- Creates portable reputation across projects
